Nvidia CEO: "An empty chair is better than a chair filled with the wrong person."

Naval Ravikant’s philosophy on quick iteration emphasizes "impatience with actions, patience with results"

Key Trade-offs
| Aspect   | Empty Chair Approach               | Trial and Error                      |
| -------- | ---------------------------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| Speed    | Slower hiring; short-term overload | Faster staffing; quicker resolution  |
| Risk     | Low long-term damage               | Potential toxicity or turnover       |
| Best for | High-stakes roles                  | Low-impact positions with easy exits |
